# Mac Admins Slack - Quick Reference

**Version:** 1.0 (Proposed)
**Last Updated:** 5 December 2025

---

## The Basics

- Be respectful and professional
- Help others; credit their work
- Stay on-topic; use threads where expected
- Search before asking

## Do Not

- Harass, spam, or troll
- Share others' private information
- Send unsolicited sales or recruiting messages
- Impersonate others or hide your affiliation

## Need Help?

- **DM any admin** - listed monthly in #announce
- **Mention @admins** - for urgent matters in public channels
- **Email:** admin@macadmins.org
